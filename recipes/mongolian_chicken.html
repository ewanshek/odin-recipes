Mongolian Chicken

Description

This Mongolian chicken is crispy slices of chicken breast stir fried in a sweet and savory sauce. A restaurant favorite that tastes even better when you make it at home!

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup of cornstarch
  • cooking oil
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 5 slices of ginger
  • red chilli
  • 4 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbs cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • green onion

Steps

  1. Mix chicken with cornstarch.
  2. Fry in the pan.
  3. Add garlic, ginger and chilli to the pan for 20s.
  4. Add hoisin sauce, soy sauce, brown sugar and sesame oil and mix.
  5. Add cornstarch water.
  6. Add chicken.
